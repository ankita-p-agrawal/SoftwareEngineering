PROJECT DESCRIPTION


Our mission is to develop the adequate technical tool for patients to receive treatments that are specific to their individual needs, dietary habits, lifestyle, exercise regimen, and personal goals in managing their diabetes. Many of today's treatments given to patients by doctors are too general and broad. Treatments should be focused with patient precision, which is what we aim to accomplish with our platform. Overall, the Harexi platform will work with you to make sure you are getting appropriate preventive care and screening to live long, healthy lives.
